Vision

Moojin Co., Ltd. aims to support easier understanding of construction by providing open-source hardware-based construction measurement devices, operational services, and research services, enabling people to access various devices anytime.

Mission

Moojin’s mission is to innovate accessible, affordable, and reliable construction technologies using open-source systems. We are committed to advancing infrastructure quality through smart sensing, data analytics, and practical engineering solutions that empower engineers and communities alike.

Moojin system generates valuable outputs essential for pavement management, including distress maps, spatial distribution maps, and automated analytical reports. These outputs are derived from both surface-level and subsurface data, offering a multi-dimensional view of pavement conditions. The distress maps highlight specific areas of deterioration, such as cracks, ruts, patching or potholes, while spatial maps provide geo-referenced visualizations that support precise asset tracking. The automated reports synthesize raw data into actionable insights, streamlining decision-making for maintenance prioritization and budget allocation. This functionality makes Moojin's technology particularly effective for LCPMS, where localized and data-driven pavement condition monitoring is critical..

Moojin offers a low-cost pavement evaluation technology in Korea, delivering a comprehensive detection system for infrastructure assessment. We provide advanced condition monitoring with both real-time and predictive capabilities, enabling timely and data-driven decision-making. The system is designed to be flexible, scalable and optimized, adapting to various field conditions while maintaining high accuracy. Overall, it serves as a more comprehensive pavement evaluation tool, pushing the boundaries of traditional monitoring methods.
